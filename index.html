A History

David Papa began music in 2006 in middle school and took on several instruments and styles ever since. Music brought David through the thicks and thins of life and brought him closer to many different people because of it. He currently has one tester album out on iTunes, Forks for Breakfast, released in 2012, and an upcoming album called West Valley Bars. This album will test out David's presence in hip-hop and give an insight on the struggles of his stories of life and love.
