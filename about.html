Process

My inspiration for this particular website stemmed from a long-scrolling site for a cupcake shop, where the banner on top was made to look like a roof of a cupcake shop or stand, and the cupcakes move along a conveyor belt when you scroll. I wanted to impliment the same idea where I enlighten people of some of the different types of instruments and tools I use to make music to inspire or aid others.
